Need Help With Your Crypto.com Account?

If you're experiencing login issues, delayed transactions, or need assistance with your wallet, use the form below to contact our support team. All responses will be sent via email.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. I can't log in to my account — what should I do?

Ensure your login details are correct. Try resetting your password via the Crypto.com app. If you still face issues, contact us using the form above.

2. I lost access to 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication)

If you’ve lost your authentication device, submit a support request with proof of ID. We’ll help you reset 2FA.

3. Why is my withdrawal delayed?

Withdrawals may be delayed due to network congestion or additional security checks. You’ll be notified via email once it’s processed.

4. My transaction shows “Pending” for too long

Sometimes network verification takes longer. If it exceeds 2 hours, contact us with your transaction ID.

Security Notice

We will never ask for your password or seed phrase. Only communicate through this official help page and your registered email address. All inquiries are answered within 24–48 hours.
